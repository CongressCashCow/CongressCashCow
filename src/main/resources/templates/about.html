About Milk-Money

Keeping track of the stock market activity can be a daunting task for the average American. The Milk-Money application was created to make it easier for individuals to stay up-to-date on the latest hot trades made by US Congress politicians.

What is Milk-Money?

Milk-Money is a web-based application that allows users to easily follow stock market activity made by US Congress politicians. The application provides the latest updates each politician's trades, allowing users to stay informed on the latest market trends and changes.

Why use Milk-Money?

  • To stay informed on the latest stock market activity made by US Congress politicians.
  • To follow the trading activity of each politician, who may have also an impact on the economy.
  • To learn more about investing in the stock market and how politicians may influence market trends.

How does Milk-Money work?

Milk-Money uses legal available data to track (stalk) the stock market activity of politicians in the US Congress. The application provides the latest updates on trades made by politicians, including the type of stock, the number of shares traded, and the date of the transaction. Users can search for specific politicians or view the latest trades made by all politicians.

Who can use Milk-Money?

Milk-Money is available to anyone who wants to stay informed on the stock market activity made by US Congress politicians. The application is designed to be user-friendly and accessible to individuals of all experience levels.

Getting started with Milk-Money

To get started with Milk-Money, simply create an account on our website. Once you have an account, you can start following the latest trades made by politicians in the US Congress.
